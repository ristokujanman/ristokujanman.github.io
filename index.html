Hi there—and thank you for stopping by! I’m Risto, a designer from Finland, currently living in Tokyo ^-^ Since 2023, I work at Fontworks, where I design and produce original Latin typefaces—both library fonts and custom ones—and give feedback to my Japanese colleagues on Latin type and rōmaji sets. I’ve had two mentors during the past two years in Tokyo: Joachim Müller-Lancé, for Latin and the Japanese writing system in general, and Akira Kobayashi, for the fundamentals of kanji. Previously, I attended the

master of design

royal academy of art, the hague

ba in graphic design

from the same art academy in The Hague. For my graduation work at Type and Media I drew Arctica—a type family that plays with the tension between high contrast and brutalism. I've also worn a different hat as an urban planning consultant, helping create more bicycle-friendly cities. I earned a
