Hi there—and thanks for stopping by! I’m Risto, a designer from Finland, now living in Tokyo. Since 2023, I’ve been working at Fontworks, where I design original Latin typefaces—both library and custom —and review Latin and rōmaji sets by my Japanese colleagues. I’ve also drawn kanji for the upcoming Tsukiji Ni-go Mincho.

In Tokyo, I’ve been fortunate to learn from two great mentors: Joachim Müller-Lancé (Latin and Japanese writing systems) and Akira Kobayashi (kanji fundamentals).

Before that, I studied type design in the

master of design

royal academy of art, the hague
