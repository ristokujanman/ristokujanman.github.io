Hi there—and thank you for stopping by! I’m Risto, a designer from Finland, currently living in Tokyo ^-^ Since 2023, I work at Fontworks, where I design and produce original Latin typefaces—both library fonts and custom ones—and give feedback to my Japanese colleagues on Latin type and rōmaji sets. I’ve also drawn kanji for the upcoming Tsukiji Ni-go Mincho.

I've had two mentors in Tokyo: Joachim Müller-Lancé, for Latin and the Japanese writing system in general, and Akira Kobayashi, for the fundamentals of kanji. Previously, I attended the master of design program, Type and Media, at the royal academy of art, the hague in The Netherlands and assisted in the production of Rizoma for R-Typography as well as ABC Maxi for Dinamo. I also received a ba in graphic design from the same art academy in The Hague.

For my graduation work at Type and Media I drew Arctica—a type family that plays with the tension between high contrast and brutalism. I've also worn a different hat as an urban planning consultant, helping create more bicycle-friendly cities. I earned a master of science in transportation engineering and urban planning at aalto university in Finland.

Outside of type, I make music, do power yoga, enjoy Japanese food (a lot), go bouldering now and then, and yes— I check my email regularly. Feel free to get in touch, or just say hi!
