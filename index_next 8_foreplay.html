Bibliomania

Publication Design, 2016

A catalogue showcasing ten of our favourite books. The publication was exhibited at Offprint Paris, Autumn 2016. Collaboration with design students Rhodé Tavenier, Zuzanna Zgierska, Jungyeon Sung and Beatrice Borgström at the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague.

Dialogue

Installation, 2017

Work about the social aspects of virtual and augmented realities. Because of the ease of surveillance by a faceless third party, is there such a thing as a private, digitally mediated dialogue? And more importantly — are we still able to share the most intimate matters with our friends, or have we started to filter ourselves without us even noticing it?
Sound by Vesa-Matti Kivioja.
